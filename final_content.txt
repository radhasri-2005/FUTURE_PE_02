## SECTION 5 — VIDEO PRODUCTION NOTES

### Shot List (10–15 second video)

| Second | Shot | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| 0–2s | Hook shot | Creator looking at camera, says hook line |
| 2–5s | B-roll 1 | Coffee pour, latte art, or cold brew close-up |
| 5–8s | B-roll 2 | Café interior — window seat, cozy corner, warm lighting |
| 8–11s | Proof shot | Creator at table with coffee, natural reaction |
| 11–13s | Product CU | Final product shot — coffee in hand, logo visible |
| 13–15s | CTA end card | Text overlay with CTA + handle |

### Tools for Video Creation

| Tool | Purpose | Link |
|------|---------|------|
| CapCut | Edit, captions, transitions | capcut.com |
| Runway | AI video generation | runwayml.com |
| Pika Labs | Text-to-video for B-roll | pika.art |
| ElevenLabs | AI voiceover | elevenlabs.io |

### Recommended Video Specs
- **Format:** 9:16 vertical (1080x1920)
- **Duration:** 10–15 seconds for paid ads, 30–45 for organic reels
- **Captions:** Auto-captions ON (80% of viewers watch without sound)
- **Music:** Trending audio from Instagram/TikTok library
- **Lighting:** Natural window light preferred for authentic UGC feel
