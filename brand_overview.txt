BRAND OVERVIEW – CAFE AURA

Business Name: CafeAura  
Location: Visakhapatnam  

About Brand:
CafeAura is a modern, cozy cafe designed for students, coffee lovers, and young professionals. It offers a calm and aesthetic environment perfect for studying, relaxing, and socializing.

Unique Selling Points (USP):
- Peaceful and quiet atmosphere
- Aesthetic interior and cozy seating
- High-quality coffee
- Student-friendly environment
- Perfect for study and hangouts

Target Audience:
- College students
- Coffee enthusiasts
- Freelancers / remote workers
- Friend groups

Brand Tone:
- Casual
- Relatable
- Friendly
- Authentic (not salesy)

Content Style:
- UGC (User Generated Content)
- Real-life experiences
- Instagram Reel style
